About me

Hi! My name is Anmol Raj. I am a Graphic Designer. I believe in the power of elegant minimalism in a world increasingly saturated with garish color. I'm not going to bore you guys by saying, I've 3 Years of experience in field of Graphic designing and last year I specialized Graphic Design by CalArts (California Institute of the Arts) and right now I'm specializing UX/UI over that I'm Graphics Lead of DSC LPU(Google Developer Students Club) and worked with GDG Jalandhar (Google Developer Groups).

In a nutshell, it means that I sit at my shiny Omen Laptop all day long creating snazzy designs for cool folks from around the world, It's great!
